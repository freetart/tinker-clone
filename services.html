Product Design

Product design is not just about how it looks; it’s about how it works. Who cares if your product is on-time and within budget, if no one likes it? Great products are not defined by feature sets, bugs and up-time – – – they are defined by users and revenue.

We believe the product development process begins a single line of code is written by listening to users and analyzing the core problem the product intends to solve. We can help you avoid wasting resources by integrating user feedback and market analysis throughout the entire development process from sketches to finished releases.

We help you dial-in your product strategy and the research and documentation needed to make it a success (and look pretty good too).

  • Product Strategy & Roadmap
  • User & Market Research
  • Feature Prioritization
  • Sketches & Wireframes
  • Business & Technical Requirements
  • Branding
  • UX /UI / Visual Design
  • Prototyping & Demos
  • Customer Development

Build

We start by assembling a dedicated team of the best talent for your product. Teams sit together, eat together and work together. They sometimes even dress alike. We create a unique team culture for every product and no engineers work on more than one project at a time. We provide highly experienced project managers and quality assurance analysts to ensure we ship clean, well-documented code, on schedule. We work in two week sprints, use tools such as Trello, Slack and GitHub and provide daily status reports on progress.

We leverage open source libraries and third party APIs whenever possible to deliver quality products as quickly and cost-efficiently as possible.

Our team of 100+ full-stack engineers all bring unique technical skills to the table. We can work with almost every modern technology, programming language and framework.
